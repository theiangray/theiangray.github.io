My name is Jan-Lukas Else and I am a 23 year old tech enthusiast with a deep passion for all things programmable. My journey into the world of bits and bytes began in elementary school when my parents gave me my first PC. But I wasn’t interested in games, I was more fascinated by the possibilities of a computer and soon started to experiment with small programs in Visual C++.

The motivation to solve everyday problems led me to participate successfully in the Jugend forscht competition several times. My last project also brought me closer to programming and I started to learn Java on my own by developing a handwriting recognition program (before every smartphone had this feature built in). For the next few years, I mainly focused on developing Android apps, some of which I published on Google Play.

After graduating from high school, I started studying computer science at the Ostfalia University of Applied Sciences in cooperation with a company in my hometown, which I finished in March 2021. In January 2020 I finished my apprenticeship as an IT specialist for application development. My focus of interest has shifted from Android application development to backend and web services.

Besides being interested in technology, I also like to travel (preferably by train), do bodyweight training and try to live a minimalist and eco-friendly lifestyle.
